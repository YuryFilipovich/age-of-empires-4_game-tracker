    FAQ

    Here are some frequently asked questions (this section will be continuously updated):

    1. How is this different from Aoe4world?

    • You have all match data available in one place (each player rank, win rate, pick rate and etc.), no need to open each player and check their stats.
    • Offline/ online mode for the top rm1x1 players from the leaderboard.
    • Also possible to track your friends' games (the app was originally created for this)
    • New features will come later :)

    How To Use

    Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use the application:

    • Do nothing -_-. If someone from list on the left is online, you will see the all the match data
    • If you/ your friend/ someone you don't know is playing a game right now, then you can paste user's id/ link from Aoe4world
    • Click 'Search' button
    • A new window with the match data for that player will be opened in new tab
    • Easy to share that link with anyone you want
